What is ASO?

ASO is a marketing process that makes mobile apps more visible on platforms such as the App Store and Google Play Store.

2. App Name

App name is the brand name of your app reflects its identity and is the first the most important impression is one of the elements.

✓ The app name is up to 50 in the Play Store and App Store should be 30 characters.

✓ Must be original and catchy.

✓ Keywords should be used.

3. Short Description

It is the second most important area in ASO and besides its direct impact on Search, it also affects the conversion rate of downloads.

✓ Short description at most, 80 in the Play Store and App Store must be 30 characters.

✓ Should include the purpose of the app.

✓ Targeted keywords should be used.

4. Description

It should tell you what your app is about and why it should be downloaded.

✓ Description should be maximum 4000 characters.
✓ It should be approached with a solution for the user’s needs.
✓ Contain original text for users important
✓ Targeted keywords should be used.

5. App Icon

The app icon is the most important visual element with the biggest impact when users are both browsing the store and searching.

✓ The app icon should be attractive, simple and understandable.

✓ The icon should represent your application.

7. Reviews & Ratings

Ratings and reviews provide important information to potential users about the quality of your app.

✓ Positive reviews and high ratings increases the credibility of your app.

✓ Addressing bad reviews and responding to user feedback it is important to respond.

8. Updates and Improvements

Updates and improvements ensure that your application remains competitive

✓ Regularly review your application to update and improve, to support users important to keep you satisfied.

✓ Descriptions of updates and new features should be emphasized.

9. Country and Language Settings

Country and Language Settings allows the user to set preferred geographic region and language options within a device or app.

✓ App stores should have appropriate country and language settings.

✓ App content should be appropriate to the language and cultural expectations of the target audience.

✓ Observe your competitors’ ASO tactics and understand what kind of keywords they are using
